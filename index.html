Our solution

This is Vollce V1™, by far the most sustainable solution to the fastly changing society when it comes to mobility. The Vollce V1™ is developed following high Dutch quality standards. This ensures a state of the art and durable product. Nevertheless, our V1 is the most affordable product in the current market. We enable future customers to choose a serious sustainable alternative.

About us

Vollce™ is an innovative electric mobility company based in Amsterdam. It is our ambition to create resourceful products that reinforce the current market. Our first product will be introduced in Q1 2020. Founded in 2019, based in Amsterdam.
